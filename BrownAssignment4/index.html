Head Jingler

A head jingler is responsible for:

  • Jingle maintenance
  • Jingle cheer and attitude
  • Knowledge of all instruments which make jingle-y noises
If this sounds like you, please apply today!

Triangle Soloist

If you have the endurance to power through 20-40 measure triangle roll solos, then this is your time to shine! Important: If you don't do forearm workouts twice a day, don't bother applying.

Marimba Maestro

Although a marimba has a deeper, more vibrant sound than the traditional, ting-y nature of most jingle instruments, it adds a rich, velvety layer which complements the bright, striking noises of our other instruments. 2, 4, and 6 mallet grip positions available!

Finger Cymbal Aficionado

A small instrument that packs an important punch. You'll need the dexterity of a thief and the finger strength of a professional rock-climber to succeed in this position.
